PROJECT

The community of weird writers in Finland is thriving, and producing memorable stories that blur and bend genre boundaries with their unbridled flight of imagination. These publications introduce you to suomikumma, “Finnish Weird”, showcase a few of its bright stars, and also give you a couple of short stories to read. Step into the wonderful world of Finnish Weird!
